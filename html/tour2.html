Sunny Spain

About the Tour

Experience the vibrant soul of Spain on this relaxing and culture-rich adventure. Over the course of several unforgettable days, you’ll enjoy the perfect mix of sun-soaked beaches and urban exploration:

  • 🏖️ Beachside Bliss: Unwind on the golden sands of the Costa del Sol and Costa Brava, where the Mediterranean breeze and crystal-clear waters create the ultimate escape.
  • 🖼️ Barcelona Highlights: Stroll down the famous La Rambla, admire Gaudí’s masterpieces like the Sagrada Família and Park Güell, and discover the city's bohemian charm and culinary delights.
  • 🏛️ Madrid’s Majesty: Explore royal palaces, world-renowned museums like the Prado, and feel the heartbeat of Spain in the capital’s bustling plazas and flamenco nights.
  • 🍷 Local Flavor: Enjoy traditional tapas, regional wines, and vibrant local markets as you dive deep into Spain’s rich culture.

This tour offers the perfect balance of relaxation, exploration, and authentic experiences, all guided by passionate locals who bring each destination to life.
